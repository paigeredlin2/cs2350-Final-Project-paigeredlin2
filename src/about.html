About us

Hello we are Sunset Rendevous!

Our story begins in the small city of Montana called Helena. We consist of four members: our singer Fiona, the guitarist Abby, drummer Michael, and last but not least, bassist Samuel.The first time we became a group was in our highschool band class. From there we've decided to band together - literally. We don't have any grand goals of world peace - it would bring us enough joy just to bring a few people together over music.

We characterize our music style as Indie-Rock. Bands that we look up to are the following in no particular order: Glass Animals, Saint Motel, Arctic Monkeys, Florence and the Machine, Paramore, and many many more artists.

Our lives would not be the same without music or our audience.

-Sunset Rendevous
