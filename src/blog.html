Performing at the Washington Theatre

It was such an amazing experience to finally meet the city of Portland, Oregon. This city is actually one of our highest-listening populations. So we are so honored to finally perform live for you all Portland-ers. The entire night was a blast. I don't think I saw Michael frown at all! An achievement in itself.

Thank you to everyone that showed up. While this wasn't our first show ever, it feels like the first performance where the people attending were there for us and not just live music. We are very grateful for your support and hope to be back in the city of Portland soon!

Disaster strikes at the Vivian Lounge!

Not that Fiona approves (or even knows) of this post, I find it neccessary to share about the absolute tragedy that had befallen on our innocent Fiona last Tuesday. She had set up her microphone as per usual, and after trying to make conversation with the audience. She sat back on her stool and the leg broke clean off, sending dear Fiona into the ground. We are lucky that she has no sense of shame, because after getting a new stool from an apologetic stage manager, she continued the show. Anyway~ sorry for embarassing you Fiona but I think this is more of a compliment of how dedicated you are and also how shameless.

We performed at our first festival!

We successfully convinced (bribed) our way into the 4th annual Q Festival in Renton, Washington! We were able to meet so many new people and other bands. Then after our performance a man approached us and offered us a contract to be our producer. Don't worry - we deliberated for a week and with big smiles we can announce that we are officially signed with Radio Mars Productions! We are forever thankful for this opprotunity and the Q Festival for providing us a way to reach a larger audience.
